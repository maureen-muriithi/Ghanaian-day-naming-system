What is your Akan Name?

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.

Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Learn more

Would you expect that the particular day of the week that you are or were born, can determine your first name?

Well, according to the Ghanaian culture, it is quite frequent.

In accordance to the Akan naming culture in Ghana, depending on whether you are male or female, children are given a "day name" which corresponds to the day in the week when they were born. These day names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.

This application will determine which particular day of the week you were born, and what your Akan name is.
